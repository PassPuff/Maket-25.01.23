The frame structure makes the machines Wattsan unique

Frame construction

The frame of the machine relieves vibration, especially during engraving, because the greatest moment of inertia is achieved during engraving.

It is important to keep in mind the accuracy of the machine as well.

Hull thickness

The increased enclosure thickness is designed to reduce vibration during engraving.

Wattsan machines have an enclosure thickness of 2.9 mm or greater on machines like the 1610 and larger. The extra enclosure thickness increases machine life and performance.
